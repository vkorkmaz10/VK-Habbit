# Audience: Investors

## Who They Are
Crypto investors, on-chain enthusiasts, and technical analysis followers. This audience includes individuals actively managing portfolios or tracking market movements who are technical enough to understand data-driven insights. They value transparent strategies over market noise and rely on a technical filter to navigate the ecosystem.

## What They Want
- Rational price forecasts based on cryptocurrency data and professional chart analysis.
- Real numbers and on-chain metrics, including costs, revenue, and time-saving automation insights.
- Tool recommendations with context, specifically how AI and automation can be applied to market analysis.
- Systems thinking — understanding how macro market shifts, technical data, and personal strategy connect to form a cohesive investment approach.

## How to Talk to Them
- Direct and specific. Use technical authority to provide clear strategies, not vague "consider exploring" suggestions.
- Show your work. Share real screenshots of charts, real process details, and the results of your technical süzgeç.
- Transparent energy — building and analyzing alongside them, sharing not just successes but also market "rekt" risks, errors, or personal losses.
- Challenge them: Use iddialı (ambitious) and hook-driven openings that emphasize the cost of ignoring rational data in favor of hype.
